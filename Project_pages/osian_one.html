About the Project

The name says it all and it is definitely a one of a kind project. Based on the three pillars of life: BODY MIND and SOUL, we realised why our living spaces should be any different. Osian One is the brain child of this realisation. It is uniquely energised to help find the peace and harmony you deserve. Situated in a prime location in Central Chennai, it is easily connected to Rail and Road networks.

The Body
Body is the physical presence of a living soul. For many, body is the container that stores these energies and helps drive one’s life. It is this physical entity that determines the span of a being and during this course of time, it is our responsibility to take care of this container we call our body. Your body deserves the pampering of a king. To ensure you get the best of facilities, we have a wide variety of features and amenities that will take care of your need so your body can rest.

The Mind
“The Mind is its own place, and in itself can make a heaven of hell, or a hell of heaven….” this immortal passage from Milton’s Paradise Lost encapsulates how our state of mind can have influence on our state of being. It is therefore imperative that we ensure the mind finds the same relaxation and energy as the body. For this very purpose, we have concierge services to take care of your everyday needs so your mind can be at peace.

The Soul
If your body is a vessel, your soul is the content in it. Your soul's true nature will at all time reflect on the person’s character. But the external factors can sometimes disturb the peace that the soul holds. It is therefore very important to live in a calm and peaceful environment that wouldn’t hinder your presence. Many believe that the souls would go back to heaven one day. But why not bring the heaven down for the souls to live on earth. At Osian One, we have created a heaven for your Mind, Body and Soul.
