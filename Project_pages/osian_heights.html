About the Project

Osian Heights is our first projects as we ventured into the Real Estate Realm. We aimed to make a home lover’s dream into a reality. With three apartment towers in a 2 acre development with 16 floors, at Osian Heights, we’ve ensured that all comforts that a person’s lifestyle demand is available at ease. Today, Osian Heights with a 3 lakh Sqft of construction and world class amenities stands as the tallest tower in North Chennai.

The entire project was completed and handed over to the apartment association by 2010. Located in a prime location, it is well connected to railways and roadways. Easily visible from distance, it stands as a majestic structure supporting lives and in tandem with the environment. It was built to be at the pinnacle of excellence in design, aesthetics and engineering.
